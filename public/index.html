SUMMARY
Neuromancer is a 1984 science fiction novel by American-Canadian writer William Gibson. It was Gibson's debut novel and the beginning of the Sprawl trilogy. The novel tells the story of a washed-up computer hacker hired by a mysterious employer to pull off the ultimate hack.
